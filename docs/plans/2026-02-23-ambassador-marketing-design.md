# ResumeLab Ambassador Marketing Plan

## Overview

Launch an evergreen "Success Story" ambassador program where college students who recently landed internships/jobs promote ResumeLab to their peer network. Zero cash burn — ambassadors are compensated with free credits only.

## Approach: Success Story Ambassadors

Ambassadors don't promote ResumeLab directly — they share their job search story, and ResumeLab is naturally part of it. Their real success (landing at a recognizable company) is the proof that makes the product sell itself.

---

## 1. Ambassador Recruiting Pipeline

### Who to Target

Students who posted on LinkedIn in the last 1-3 months about landing an internship/job. Keywords to search:
- "I'm excited to share that I'll be joining..."
- "Thrilled to announce my internship at..."
- "After months of applications, I finally..."

### Qualifying Criteria
- Posted publicly about landing a role (proves they're comfortable sharing)
- Has 200+ LinkedIn connections (minimum reach)
- Ideally at a recognizable company (Google, Amazon, Goldman — the brand name does the selling)

### DM Script (LinkedIn)
> Hey [Name], congrats on [Company]! I'm building ResumeLab — an AI tool that scores and rewrites resumes. Since you just went through the whole process, I'd love to give you free unlimited access. In exchange, if you liked it, would you be open to sharing your experience with your network? No pressure, just a genuine offer.

### Volume
- DM 30-50 students per week
- Expect ~20-30% acceptance rate
- Target: 10-20 ambassadors in month 1, then 5-10 new per month

---

## 2. Ambassador Kit & Onboarding

Each accepted ambassador receives:

### Free Credits
300 credits loaded into their account (Pro tier value, costs ~$0.60 in API calls).

### Unique Referral Code
Format: `FIRSTNAME-COLLEGE` (e.g., `RAHUL-IITD`, `SARAH-STANFORD`).
- New users enter the code at signup for 10 bonus credits on top of free tier
- Tracked manually in a Google Sheet

### Ambassador Playbook (1-page doc)
- How ResumeLab works (so they can explain it)
- 3 suggested post templates they can customize
- Their unique code + how to share it
- What they earn: 50 bonus credits per referral who signs up

### Before/After Content
Encourage ambassadors to:
1. Run their own resume through ResumeLab
2. Screenshot the score (e.g., "My resume scored 68... then after AI rewrite, 91")
3. Use this as the hook for their post

---

## 3. Content Strategy

### Post Template 1: The Score Reveal
> I ran my resume through an AI analyzer before internship season... it scored 64/100. Turns out my bullets were all "responsible for" statements. After the AI rewrite: 89/100. I used that version to apply to [Company] and got the offer.
> If you're applying this season, try it free: [link] (use code FIRSTNAME-COLLEGE for bonus credits)

### Post Template 2: The Advice Post
> 3 things I wish I knew before internship applications:
> 1. Your resume gets scanned by ATS software before a human sees it
> 2. Recruiters spend 6 seconds on your resume
> 3. "Responsible for X" gets ignored — "Increased X by 40%" gets interviews
> I used ResumeLab to fix all of this. Free to try: [link]

### Post Template 3: The Before/After
> Before: "Worked on backend development using Python"
> After: "Built REST APIs serving 50K daily requests with 99.9% uptime, reducing response time by 40%"
> Same experience. Completely different impression. [ResumeLab] did this rewrite in 30 seconds.

### Why These Work
- Lead with value (career advice), not a sales pitch
- The ambassador's real success is the proof
- College peers trust peers more than any ad

## 4. Distribution Channels

| Channel | How | Why |
|---------|-----|-----|
| LinkedIn | Posts with score screenshots | Highest reach, professional context, job posts go viral |
| WhatsApp Groups | College batch groups, placement groups | Intimate, high-trust, daily engagement |
| Instagram Stories | Quick screenshot of score + "link in bio" | Casual reach, catches people scrolling |

Not recommended yet: Twitter/X (low college engagement), Reddit (looks spammy), Email (too formal for peer sharing).

---

## 5. Tracking & Metrics

### Google Sheet Columns
| Ambassador | Code | College | Company | Signups | Credits Earned | Date Joined |

### Targets

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 3 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Active ambassadors | 10-15 | 30-50 |
| Signups via referral codes | 50-100 | 300-500 |
| Conversion to paid | 10-15% | 15-20% |
| Revenue from referrals | $50-150 | $500-1000 |
| Cost | $0 | $0 |

### Key Leading Indicator
Number of ambassador posts per week. If ambassadors aren't posting, the funnel dies. Follow up weekly.

### Scale Signal
If Month 1 shows >5% of referral signups converting to paid, that's the signal to invest more — recruit aggressively, consider building in-app referral tracking, or add cash bounties.

---

## 6. Launch Sequence

### Week 1: Setup
- Create Google Sheet tracker
- Write ambassador playbook (1-page doc)
- Set up manual referral code process (add bonus credits to accounts)
- Prepare 3 post templates

### Week 2: Recruit First 10
- Search LinkedIn for recent internship announcements
- DM 30-50 students
- Onboard accepted ambassadors: give credits, code, playbook

### Week 3: First Wave of Posts
- Nudge ambassadors to post their first content
- Share examples of what's working in a group chat
- Track signups

### Week 4: Iterate
- Check metrics: which ambassadors drive signups?
- Double down on what's working (which post style, which channel)
- Recruit 5 more ambassadors based on learnings

### Ongoing (Monthly)
- Recruit 5-10 new ambassadors from fresh LinkedIn internship posts
- Retire inactive ambassadors (no posts in 30 days)
- Share "top ambassador" highlights to create friendly competition

---

## Key Decisions
- **Compensation:** Free credits only (zero cash burn)
- **Recruiting:** LinkedIn DM outreach to recent internship announcers
- **Tracking:** Google Sheet + manual referral codes (no in-app features)
- **Content:** 3 post templates — score reveal, advice post, before/after
- **Channels:** LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Instagram Stories
- **Timeline:** 4-week rolling launch, then ongoing monthly recruiting
